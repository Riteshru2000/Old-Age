BUSES FREE RIDE

Most counties across the World offer free or low-cost public transportation services designed for seniors who need door-to-door rides .in England also,A National Bus Pass was introduced for people aged 60 years and older in England in 2006, entitling holders to free local bus travel in their area of residence after 9:30 am on weekdays, and all day on weekends and public holidays.Even UBER Drivers give discount to elder people's.

KEEP Wheelchair

addition to providing mobility, an appropriate wheelchair benefits the physical health and quality of life of the users by helping in reducing common problems such as pressure sores and improve respiration and digestion. There are many types of wheelchairs with varying features and benefits, One of them is Power Wheelchair. Power wheelchairs have a motor and rechargeable battery and are controlled using a joystick on the armrest, requiring only minimal effort from the user. Electric and manual wheelchairs both allow the user to get around and undertake daily activities without assistance.

Exercise

Exercise produces endorphins (“feel good” hormone), which act as a stress reliever and leaves you feeling happy and satisfied. Exercise improves strength and flexibility. It may improve cognitive function (how your brain works), Activities like Sudoku or crossword puzzles can help keep your brain active, but little comes close to the beneficial effects of exercise on the brain. Regular exercise helps increase your metabolism and build muscle mass, helping your body to burn more calories.
